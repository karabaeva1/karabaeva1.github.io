никита

Hello! My name is Nikita, I am 12 years old. I want to tell you about my favourite pet. I have a cat. My cat’s name is Murzik and he is 5 years old. My parents gave me a kitten when I went to the first class. The kitten was small, because of this he was afraid and meowed loudly. I cuddled him and put him on my lap. Then he was purring, that’s why I named him Murzik. At present Murzik is a big, fluffy and beautiful cat. Usually he eats in the kitchen, walks around the flat or sleeps on my bed. He doesn’t go outside; he is a house cat. On the evenings I play with my cat, pet and cuddle him. When I am at home, Murzik is always near me. He is my best friend and I love him very much.
